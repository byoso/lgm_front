Quick Markdown Guide

RPGAdventure.eu allows to use simple text as well as the markdown language in its items and PCs descriptions. Markdown is a powerfull tool that requires only a little knowledge to do very cool stuffs, here are some examples


Titles

A '#' marks a title, the more '#' you put, the smaller the title will be. 

# Bigest Title 1
## Title 2
### Title 3
#### Title 4
##### Title 5
###### Smallest Title 6


Lists

The lists are marked with "-" or "*", an indentation is 4 spaces.

- Item 1
- Item 2
  - Item 3
  - Item 4
    - Item 5
    - Item 6

Tables

| param 1 | param 2 | param 3 |
|--|--|--|
| value 1 | value 2 | value 3 |
| value 4 | value 5 | value 6 |

Links and images

You can easily include links and images with markdown


**link**:

[link text](https://www.example.com)

**image:** (same with a "!" just before the link)

![Image](https://i.goopics.net/lhw9s2.png)

**link with image:**

[![Image](https://i.goopics.net/lhw9s2.png)](https://www.example.com)
